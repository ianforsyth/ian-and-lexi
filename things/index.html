The Best of Durango

Durango Hot Springs

The Durango Hot springs are tucked into a beautiful mountain resort and spa with multiple pools where you can enjoy a soak, a drink, a massage, and more!

MORE INFO

Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad

This classic Durango excursion departs from Downtown and journeys through Cascade Canyon, offering magnificent views of the Animas River and the snowclad peaks of the Rocky Mountains of southwest Colorado. (If you have time to take the drive from Durango to Silverton, the views are even better from the top!)

MORE INFO

Mesa Verde

Mesa Verde National Park is a truly unique place where ancient cliff dwellings are built directly into towering canyon walls, offering a rare glimpse into Ancestral Puebloan life. Experience sweeping desert views, winding mesa-top roads, and stone cities carved into rock.

MORE INFO

Hikes & Bikes

Durango is surrounded by easy-access hiking and biking trails that make this place so special. Cruise up Coal Bank for a beautiful alpine adventure, or pop over to Horse Gulch for great views of the town. Rent a mountain bike from one of the many local shops for a real taste of local Durango action.
