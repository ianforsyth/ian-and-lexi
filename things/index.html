The Best of Durango

Durango Hot Springs

Beautiful mountain resort and spa with multiple pools where you can enjoy a soak, a drink, a massage, and more!

Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad

Embark on an incredible journey through the San Juan Mountains! This unforgettable excursion departs from Durango and journeys through Cascade Canyon, offering magnificent views of the Animas River and the snowclad peaks of the Rocky Mountains of southwest Colorado.

DETAILS

Mesa Verde

Mesa Verde National Park is a truly unique place where ancient cliff dwellings are built directly into towering canyon walls, offering a rare glimpse into Ancestral Puebloan life. With sweeping desert views, winding mesa-top roads, and stone cities carved into rock, it feels equal parts history, adventure, and awe.

DETAILS

Hikes & Bikes

Durango is surrounded by easy-access hiking and biking trails that make this place so special. Cruise up Coal Bank for a beautiful alpine adventure, or pop over to Horse Gulch for great views of the town. Rent a mountain bike from one of the many local shops for a real taste of local Durango action.
